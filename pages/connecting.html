CONNECTING
Trusted networks are crucial to navigating complex topics and getting the most out of any analysis or research output.

We maintain a Slack team to promote community building to further the discourse between groups and promote the best possible outcomes in data analysis projects and efforts.

DISCUSSION

Our Slack team is one method of communication for our Applied Data Analytics program. We include channels for programming specific issues (eg Python and SQL), training program specific topics, and general discussion. If you are interested in joining, please send us an email describing your background, how you can contribute to the discussion, and why you want to join.
